About Me

Hi, I’m Ramona – a voiceover artist with a compelling, spirited voice that’s bursting with charisma! I have the energy and enthusiasm to keep your listener engaged from start to finish. Authenticity is hard to come by, especially in the marketing world, but the right voice can make your script more believable. Listeners often describe my voice as ‘the everywoman’ because it likely reminds you of someone you know – a busy mom, your upbeat friend, or a youthful teacher. I have a knack for bundling crystal-clear diction and performative inflection in a believable, down-to-earth voice that feels like home. Teamwork makes the dream work. I put in the time to understand the ins-and-outs of every brand I partner with. My informative and trustworthy tone is backed by detailed research. Plus, I’m a go-with-the-flow kind of gal. You deserve an easy-going, creative partner at every step.
